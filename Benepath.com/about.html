Our Insurance Expertise

Benepath was founded in 2004 as a consulting firm helping consumers and businesses navigate the insurance marketplace. Over two decades, we've expanded from health insurance into a full suite of coverage types — connecting shoppers with local independent advisors who know their whate's market inside and out. Whether you're a first-time buyer or switching providers, we make finding the right coverage simple and free.
