Why You Need Insurance

Owning a daycare or childcare business comes with a lot of responsibility and we all make mistakes so it is important to protect yourself and your business from lawsuits due to oversights. Professional mistakes can cost your business due to a client being dissatisfied with undelivered services or negligence.

What It Covers:

Work Mistakes

If your business makes a mistake that ends up costing your client money, then your business is covered if you must pay for the client’s losses.

Attorney fees and court costs and settlements

Your business is covered in the event of a lawsuit from a client, regardless of fault.

Negligence/Undelivered Services

If your work does not meet standards, such as giving incorrect advice or you did not finish work that was supposed to be completed by a specific time, then you are covered if a lawsuit ensues.
