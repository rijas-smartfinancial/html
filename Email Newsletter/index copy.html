As we step into spring, it's the perfect time to clear out the clutter and make room for a fresh start- at home, at work, and in your mindset. A clean, organized space doesn't just look better-it can boost productivity, reduce stress, and spark a sense of renewal. Here are simple, effective ways to declutter your home, workspace, and headspace this season.

Spring Cleaning Your Workstation

A cluttered workspace can drain your focus and slow your productivity. Give your desk a spring refresh with these steps:

Declutter & Purge

Sort through papers, sticky notes, and miscellaneous items. If you haven't used it in months, it's time to toss or file it.

Wipe Down Everything

Dust off your desk, keyboard, and monitor. Disinfect high-touch surfaces to keep germs at bay.

Go Digital

Delete unnecessary files, organize folders, and unsubscribe from junk emails to streamline your digital workspace.

Refresh Your Setup

Add a plant, update your screensaver, or use a new notebook to bring fresh energy to your space.

Spring Cleaning at Home

Your home is your sanctuary-and a deep clean can make it feel brand new. Start with these simple refreshes:

Clear Out Closets & Drawers

Donate clothes you no longer wear and reorganize storage spaces.

Tidy Kitchen &
Pantry

Toss expired food, wipe down shelves, and deep clean appliances.

Freshen Up Living Spaces

Vacuum, dust, and swap out winter decor for lighter, spring-friendly touches.

Refresh Your
Air

Open windows, replace air filters, and bring in fresh flowers or essential oils for a seasonal scent.

Spring Clean Your Mindset

A fresh start isn't just about physical spaces-it's also about mental clarity. Try these simple ways to reset:

Streamline Your Schedule

Prioritize what's important by letting go of nonessential tasks.

Practice Gratitude

Keep a journal or take a moment each day to appreciate the positive things in your life.

Set New Goals

Spring is a great time to revisit New Year's resolutions or set fresh intentions for the months ahead.

A little spring cleaning can go a long way in creating a more productive, peaceful, and inspiring environment. Whether it's your home, your workspace, or your mindset-start fresh this season and enjoy the benefits of a clean slate!

Looking Ahead
6. If you had to sum up your vision for the company's financial future in one sentence, what would it be?

Maximize our share of wallet across lead, click, and call monetization while never forgetting who our customers are and how to be relentless about adding value for them. I think performance will come if you are religious about product innovation and helping your customers succeed and rather than have financial targets in a vacuum (e.g. "be a billion dollar company"), it's much better to focus on what you're delivering. From that, you'll win business, you'll see clients telling you what to build, you'll catch up to and surpass competitors who lack the same drive, etc.

7. What's one message you'd like to share with employees as we move forward into 2025?
