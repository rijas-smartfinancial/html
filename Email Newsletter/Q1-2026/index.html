At SmartFinancial, we're committed to helping our employees feel confident about their financial future. We were also excited to introduce our 401(k) advisor through SFP Wealth, Ryan Curley, who is available to support employees with personalized, one-on-one guidance tailored to individual financial goals.

What is a 401(k)?

A 401(k) is a retirement savings plan that allows you to set aside money for your future while taking advantage of important tax benefits. It's one of the most effective tools available to help build long-term financial security.

How it works

With a 401(k), you choose a percentage of your paycheck to contribute, and those contributions are automatically deducted each pay period—making saving simple and consistent. Your contributions are then invested, giving your money the opportunity to grow over time.

Why it matters

Starting early and contributing consistently can have a significant impact thanks to the power of compound growth. This means you're earning returns not only on what you contribute, but also on the earnings those contributions generate—helping your savings grow faster over time.

Even small contributions can add up, and gradually increasing your contribution rate can make a meaningful difference in your long-term financial readiness.

Maximize Your Benefit

Even more importantly, SmartFinancial offers a company match to help boost your savings. The company matches 50% of your contributions up to 3% of your pay. That means if you contribute 3% or more, you’ll receive the full 1.5% match—essentially free money added to your account just for participating.

Taking full advantage of the match is one of the easiest ways to accelerate your retirement savings.
