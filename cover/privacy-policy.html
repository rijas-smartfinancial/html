Cover.com Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

Your privacy is a priority at Cover.com. This Privacy Policy explains how Cover Technologies, LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles your Personal Information across our websites, applications, and digital engagements (collectively, the “Services”).

By interacting with our Services, you consent to the practices detailed here and in our Terms of Use. If you do not agree with these terms, please refrain from using our Site.

1. INSURANCE DISCLAIMER

Cover.com is a platform designed to connect you with insurance providers; we are not an insurance carrier or a licensed agent. We do not charge users a fee for submitting inquiries. Instead, we receive compensation from the insurance companies, agents, and third-party service providers (“Insurance Services”) that we partner with to provide you with coverage options.

2. Information Categories We Collect

We collect "Personal Information"—data that identifies or relates to you—depending on how you use our Services:

  • Identifiers: Name, date of birth, Social Security Number, driver’s license number, and login credentials.
  • Contact Info: Mailing address, email, phone, and fax numbers.
  • Commercial Data: Information on owned/leased vehicles, residency, insurance claim history, and desired coverage.
  • Financial Details: Credit card or bank account numbers used for transactions.
  • Consumer Reports: Driving history, criminal records, and credit-based insurance scores.
  • Protected Characteristics: Gender, marital status, and other demographic data.
  • Employment & Health: Occupation, education level, medical history, and tobacco use.
  • Digital Activity: IP addresses, browser types, device information, and site interaction logs.
  • Inferences: Profiles regarding your preferences, behavior, and predispositions.
  • Call & Session Recording. Call content and activity that you undertake while on our website or the websites of our Service Providers may be recorded, saved and referenced for informational and quality control purposes.

3. Collection Methods

We gather data through:

  • Voluntary Submission: Information you enter into forms, surveys, or through direct communication.
  • Automated Technology: We use cookies (Session, Persistent, Essential, and Advertising), pixels, and clear GIFs to track site performance and user engagement.
  • Third-Party Sources: We supplement our records with data from insurance agents, carriers, and consumer reporting agencies.

4. Use of Your Information

We utilize Personal Information to:

  • Deliver the Site and fulfill your requested insurance inquiries.
  • Communicate updates, marketing offers, and service notices via mail, email, phone, or text.
  • Enhance our Services through data analytics and personalized content.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements.
  • Automated Decisions: We may use automated systems to streamline service delivery and offer relevant products.

5. Sharing and Selling Data

To facilitate our business and your insurance search, we sell and/or share data with:

  • Service Providers: Trustworthy vendors who assist with hosting, IT, legal, and accounting.
  • Third-Party Marketing: We may sell your information to insurance agents or carriers specifically to provide you with the quotes or offers you have requested.
  • Legal Compliance: We disclose data to law enforcement or government entities when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Corporate Transfers: In the event of a merger or sale of Cover Technologies, LLC, your data may be transferred as a business asset.
  • Clicking Through: If you have arrived at our site through “clicking through” another website, information provided by you or collected from you at that website may be transferred to us, and we may use it and/or retain it for our purposes. Please review the privacy policy from any and all websites that you use to determine what information is collected and used by that website.

Consumer Report Notice: By submitting an inquiry, you grant express consent for our partners to obtain consumer reports (including credit-based insurance scores) to determine eligibility and pricing.

Testimonials: If you submit a testimonial, you provide us permission to use it in any manner ofr any purpose, including but not limited to, marketing and advertising communications without compensation to you. We further reserve the right to post the testimonial or any portion thereof on the Site or in other advertising materials.

6. Tracking and Cookie Management

We use Google Analytics to improve our site experience and to aggregate certain information about our site visitors that is anonymized. We also include social media features/widgets such as Facebook, YouTube, linkedIn and Twitter. You can control how your data is tracked by adjusting your browser settings. For specific opt-outs, visit:

  • Google Analytics: Opt-out Tool
  • Bing/Microsoft Ads: Privacy Settings

Do Not Track: Please note that our Site does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals.

7. Your Choices and Opt-Outs

  • Marketing Emails: You can unsubscribe using the link in our commercial emails.
  • Location Tracking: Disable GPS or push notifications via your device settings.
  • General Opt-Out: Email info@cover.com to be removed from marketing lists. You will still receive transactional messages related to your specific requests.

8. Data Security and Retention

We employ commercially reasonable physical and technical safeguards to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission is entirely secure, and you use our Services at your own risk. We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill our business relationship or comply with legal and tax obligations.

9. Public Content and Third-Party Links

  • Public Forums: Information shared on our blogs or message boards is public and shared at your own risk.
  • Linked Sites: We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites linked from our platform.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Site is for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we learn we have collected data from someone under 18, we will delete it. Contact info@cover.com for assistance.

14. Right to Non-Discrimination

We value your privacy and will never discriminate against you for exercising your California privacy rights. This means we won’t deny you services or charge different prices just because you made a request.

15. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Under California law, some of our data-sharing practices—such as sharing information with advertisers to show you relevant products—may be considered a "sale" of personal information. You have the right to opt-out of this at any time.

How to Opt-Out:

  • For Automated Tracking: To stop us from sharing data collected via cookies or tracking pixels, adjust your browser settings to block cookies or notify you before they are placed. You can find more details in our Cookies and Other Technologies section.
  • For All Other Information: If you want to opt-out of us sharing your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes, please complete our "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" form located in our Privacy Center.

16. Shine the Light

Once per calendar year, California residents can request a report detailing the categories of personal information we shared with third parties for their own direct marketing purposes during the previous year.

To request this, visit our Privacy Center and select “Shine the Light Request” from the dropdown menu. Once we receive your request, we will provide the list of categories and the names/addresses of those third parties within 30 days.
